Welcome to VILA PROI

Located in picturesque Ksamil, just 1.8 miles from Butrint National Park, our apartments offer the perfect blend of comfort and convenience. With free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, fully equipped kitchens, and private balconies, each unit ensures a relaxing stay.

Explore nearby attractions like Ksamil Beach 9 and Paradise Beach, or simply unwind in our garden oasis. And with complimentary private parking and Corfu International Airport just 57 miles away, your journey here is effortless. Welcome to your home away from home at Vila Proi!
